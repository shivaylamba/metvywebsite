About Metvy

As young entrepreneurs, we had felt that enabling seamless and automated networking, and harnessing its power to get the right mentorship is a major challenge and the options available weren’t the perfect fit for many. We hence decided to solve the problem we faced ourselves.

Founded by Kirori Mal College, Delhi University and YC Startup School alumni, Metvy is India’s First Multilingual Networking and Mentorship Platform, which connects like-minded professionals and individuals with the power of Artificial Intelligence, leading to a bespoke networking and mentorship experience.

Our aim is concise and simple: Utilizing the infinite human potential present hyper-locally through an automated intuitive platform that provides need-based networking where people can find the right people to connect and get mentored from.

With the onset of the pandemic, social distancing and work from home becoming the new normal, the dire need for up-skilling and the right direction, Cohort Based Mentorship Programs by Metvy, in its true sense, have stood the test of time and represents the future of innovation and education.

Be it job opportunities, or connecting with professionals or even learning and getting mentored by Industry Experts, Metvy is the single stop for it all.
